Project overview

A deployed MLB analytics product built around live data.

SlateSignal is designed as a portfolio-quality sports analytics app: it pulls live MLB data, generates conservative matchup signals, explains the model inputs, tracks saved leans, and compares fair lines to market prices.

Problem

Sports dashboards often show numbers without context.

SlateSignal focuses on the why: team form, run profile, starter context, model confidence, and fair-line comparison in one workflow.

Build

Node app with live MLB data and a browser-first dashboard.

The app uses a small Node server, MLB Stats API data, a conservative model layer, local analyst explanations, and Render deployment.

Product path

From portfolio project to paid research tool.

The roadmap includes saved watchlists, historical odds, lineup data, alerts, and paid research reports once the analytics foundation is proven.

Methodology

How SlateSignal creates a model lean.

The model is intentionally conservative. It is built to create research signals, not certainty, and every output should be judged against historical results and market prices.

Inputs
  • Team winning percentage and Pythagorean strength
  • Recent form from last-ten records
  • Run differential, offense, and run prevention
  • Home-field adjustment
  • Probable starter ERA, WHIP, strikeouts, and workload
Calibration
  • Probabilities are regressed toward 50/50
  • Model probabilities are capped between 42% and 58%
  • Signal strength is capped to avoid false precision
  • Saved leans are settled only after MLB marks games final
Known gaps
  • No bullpen fatigue model yet
  • No weather, umpire, or park-factor layer yet
  • No confirmed batting-order model yet
  • Historical ROI needs a paid closing-odds provider

Interview angle

Why this is a strong project to talk about.

  • It has a clear audience: fans, fantasy players, sports creators, and small coaching staffs.
  • It can grow technically: data ingestion, APIs, prediction models, persistent storage, scheduled jobs, and alerts.
  • It can grow as a business: free dashboard, paid research reports, alerts, and creator tools after the model has a trustworthy record.

SlateSignal outputs are informational and experimental. They are not betting advice. The model must be reviewed against saved results and closing lines before any strategy should be trusted.
